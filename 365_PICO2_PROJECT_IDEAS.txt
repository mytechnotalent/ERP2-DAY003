## üí° Tips for Success

1.  **Start Simple** - Begin with Day 1 and build complexity gradually
2. **Document Everything** - Keep a project journal with notes and learnings
3. **Reuse Code** - Build a library of reusable functions as you progress
4.  **Combine Components** - After mastering individual components, integrate them
5. **Debug Systematically** - Use the LCD or serial output for debugging
6. **Safety First** - Be careful with relay projects involving mains voltage
7. **Explore Both Languages** - Try projects in both MicroPython and Rust
8. **Share Your Work** - Post your projects and help others learn
